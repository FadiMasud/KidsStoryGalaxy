Privacy Policy

Kids Story Galaxy · Last updated: February 14, 2026

Summary: Kids Story Galaxy does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All app data stays on your device.

1. About This App

Kids Story Galaxy is an interactive storybook app designed for children ages 3–12. It is developed by Fadi Masud and is available on the Apple App Store.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information from users, including children. Specifically:

  • No names, email addresses, or account creation required
  • No location data collected
  • No photos, contacts, or device identifiers accessed
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs included
  • No advertising of any kind
  • No third-party data-collection services

3. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only to provide its features:

  • Reading progress and earned stars
  • Coloring masterpiece images
  • Achievement badges and reading streaks
  • User preferences (language, theme, narration settings)

This data is never transmitted off your device. It can be removed by deleting the app.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Kids Story Galaxy is designed with children's privacy as a top priority. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide:

  • No personal information is collected from children (or any user)
  • No user accounts or login required
  • No social features, chat, or user-generated content shared online
  • No behavioral tracking or profiling

5. Subscriptions

Kids Story Galaxy offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions to unlock all stories. Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple through the App Store:

  • Monthly: $6.99/month
  • Quarterly: $14.99 every 3 months
  • Semi-Annual: $24.99 every 6 months
  • Annual: $34.99/year

Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions auto-renew unless turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

We do not have access to your payment information. All transactions are processed securely by Apple.

6. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is the Apple App Store for subscription processing.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
